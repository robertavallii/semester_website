ABOUT

Semester is an editorial project developed at SUPSI in Mendrisio between September 2024 and March 2025. The students began the journey by selecting design objects as a starting point for their reflections and explorations. Each participant developed an original project, delving into their chosen object through creative photography and graphic design, crafting part of the editorial artifact by merging aesthetics with storytelling.

In parallel, a group of 12 students contributed by creating installations and interactive digital projects designed to enhance and support the magazine. Semester is the outcome of this collective synergy, celebrating design as a multidimensional experience that weaves together visual language, technology, and creativity.
